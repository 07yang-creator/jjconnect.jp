Mansion Manager

Professional Apartment Management System

Making property management easier and tenant living more comfortable

Product Overview

Mansion Manager is a comprehensive management system built specifically for apartment and property management. It integrates tenant management, financial tracking, maintenance work orders, and facility booking into a single platform — helping property managers boost efficiency and optimize the tenant experience.

Whether you manage a small apartment building or a large residential community, Mansion Manager provides professional and efficient solutions that turn complex property management into a streamlined operation and significantly improve tenant satisfaction.

Core Features

👥

Tenant Management

A complete tenant information system including move-in registration, contract management, renewal reminders, and communication logs — all information at a glance.

💰

Financial Management

Automated rent collection, expense tracking, and invoice generation. Supports multiple payment methods with real-time financial reports for clear visibility into income and expenses.

🔧

Maintenance Work Orders

An online repair request system where tenants can submit maintenance requests anytime. Managers can track repair progress in real time, improving response speed and service quality.

📅

Facility Booking

Online booking management for shared amenities such as meeting rooms, gyms, and parking spaces — preventing conflicts and maximizing facility utilization.

📢

Announcements & Notifications

Send community announcements, payment reminders, and event notices with one click. Supports SMS, email, and in-app push notifications to ensure timely delivery.

📊

Data Analytics

Multi-dimensional reports on occupancy rates, revenue statistics, and maintenance trends — helping managers make data-driven decisions and optimize operations.
